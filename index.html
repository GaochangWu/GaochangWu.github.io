Aloha! Welcome to my homepage. I am now an associate professor in the State Key Laboratory of Synthetical Automation for Process Industries (SAPI), Northeastern University (China). My supervisor is Prof. Tianyou Chai. My current research interests include light field processing, machine learning and working condition recognition in industrial fields.
